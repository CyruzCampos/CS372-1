                            Welcome to Mathiac

1. Decide on which subject you wish to work on.

2. You are then given five different questions.

3. Do your best to solve each question.

4. When you are done, you will see your score.

5. Your score will be saved as a high score.

6. Try to beat your friends and best their score.

7. Try to even beat your own score.

8. Have fun and good luck mathematician!
